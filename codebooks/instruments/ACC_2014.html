Component Description

The accelerometry (ACC) section provides information on which devices were used by the participant to collect data for the accelerometry portion of the survey. This was a voluntary portion where respondents could wear one device on the hip, one device on the wrist or both (or neither if declined to participate)for seven days consecutively.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants 18+

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The ACC section was administered by an interviewer during the in-home part of the survey using a computer-assissted personal interview (CAPI) program.

Data Processing

The ACC section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of cleaning by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.
