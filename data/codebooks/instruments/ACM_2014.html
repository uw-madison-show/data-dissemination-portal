Component Description

The accelerometry for minors (ACM) section provides information on which devices were used by the participant to collect data for the accelerometry portion of the survey. This was a voluntary portion where minors were asked to wear one device on the wrist for seven days consecutively.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants 6-17 years old are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The ACM section was administered by an interviewer during the in-home part of the survey using a computer-assissted personal interview (CAPI) program.

Data Processing

The ACM section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of cleaning by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.
