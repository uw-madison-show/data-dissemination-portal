Title: History of drinking alcoholic beverage (ALQ)

Years of Content: 2013

First Published: March 2014

Last Revised: July 2015

Subject: Alcohol use

Record Source(s):

Data File: alq_2013.sas7bdat

Component Description

The ALQ focuses on respondents’ history of drinking alcoholic beverage.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The ALQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the exam center portion (Time 3) of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

The ALQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the exam center portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. Mid-way through the year, Question item ALQ120 was renamed ALQ130_R2 and Question item ALQ130 was renamed ALQ130_R2. The order of the questions was changed so that ALQ130_R2 is given before ALQ120_R2.
