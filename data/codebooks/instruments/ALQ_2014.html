Years of Content: 2014

First Published: March 2015

Last Revised: August 2015

Subject: Alcohol Use

Record Source(s):

Data File: alq_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The ALQ focuses on respondents' history of drinking alcoholic beverages.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants age 18+ are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The ALQ section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionniares provided to study participants at the end of the first in-home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The ALQ section data were systematically reviewed by logical, processing, and data input error during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

This is the first year that ALQ was a self-administered questionnaire. It changed modes this year and was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the exam center portion of the SHOW study in years past.
