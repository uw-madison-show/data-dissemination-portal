Component Description

The Anthropometry & Blood Pressure section provides information on physical measures, such as height and weight. Self-reported measurements are also included in this section.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The WHQ/ANT/BP section is completed on paper by an interviewer during Time 3 (survey center) visit. It is then entered retrospectively via computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI)

Data Processing and Editing

This section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. Numerical values for measured height, waist circumference, and hip circumference were typed twice during data entry to reduce clerical errors. In addition, repeat measurements of these values were required to be within 2 cm of each other.

Known Analytic Notes

Arm used to measure BP (BP040_ARM, BP040_MAS, BP040_INJ, BP040_DIA): This information was not recorded for part of 2008.

 

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
