Component Description

The Anthropometry & Blood Pressure section provides information on physical measures, such as height and weight. Self-reported measurements are also included in this section.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants 18 years old and older are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The WHQ/ANT/BP section is administered during the in-home visit in person with results recorded through CAPI.

Data Processing

This section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. Numerical values for measured height, waist circumference, and hip circumference were typed twice during data entry to reduce clerical errors. In addition, values from repeat measurements for each of these measures were required to be within 2 inches of each other, if that was not the case the instrument prompted the interviewer to conduct the measure for the third time. In previous years the repeat measurements were required to be within 2 centimeters (cm) of each other, but this year there was an error in the computer system, this will be changed back to 2 cm for next year.

Analytic Notes

For the first time this year, these data were collected as part of the in-home visit of the SHOW study, in previous years the data were collected during the Exam Center visit. Appropriate weights should be used when analysing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use).
