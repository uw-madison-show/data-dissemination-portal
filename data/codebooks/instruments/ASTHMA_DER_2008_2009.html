Component Description

The Asthma section includes variables on the diagnosis, awareness (undiagnosed asthma), treatment, and control of asthma. See Analytic Notes for definitions.

Analytic Notes

Asthma was defined as self-reported current asthma and previous physician diagnosis or having max spirometry reading < 80% of predicted value at Time 3 measurement. Awareness and treatment of asthma were defined for asthmatic participants based on the previous definition. Awareness of asthmatic status was based on a self report of ever being diagnosed with asthma (see HHQ210). Treatment of asthma was defined as current use of asthma medication (see HHQ217). Control of asthma among participants currently taking asthma medication was determined by comparing max FEV1 reading to predicted FEV1 reading (FEV1_PRD). The dataset contains only SPIDs who completed Time 3 (N=507). 123 SPIDs are missing a valid FEV1 reading. 2 SPIDs are missing values for HHQ210 and HHQ214.

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study and T3 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data.
