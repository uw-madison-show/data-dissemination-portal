Title: Sleep Apnea Risk Score (Berlin)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: August 2015

Last Revised: September 2015

Subject: Sleep Apnea

Raw Data Source(s): SLP, ANT_DER, HTN_DER

Data File: berlin_der_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The BERLIN section includes information on individuals' risk of having sleep apnea.

Analytic Notes

All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. SAS calculations have not been included in this codebook but are available upon request.

References

Netzer NC, Stoohs RA, Netzer CM, Clark K, Strohl KP: Using the Berlin Questionnaire to identify patients at risk for the sleep apnea syndrome. Ann Intern Med 1999, 131(7):485-491.
