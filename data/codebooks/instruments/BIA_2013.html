Component Description

The Bioimpedance Analysis (BIA) section provides information on exclusion criteria and factors that may affect the results, as well as the values of BIA testing.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section. Eligibility for BIA testing is assessed in BIA080-BIA110.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The BIA section is administered during the Exam Center visit in person with results recorded through CAPI.

Data Processing

The BIA section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
