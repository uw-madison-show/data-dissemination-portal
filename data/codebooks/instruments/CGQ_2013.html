Component Description

The caregiving section (CGQ) provides specific information regarding current or past unpaid regular care provided to a friend or family member made necessary due to disability or illness.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 were eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The CGQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the exam center portion (Time 3) of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

The CGQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the exam center portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. The 2013 caregiver portion of the survey was altered starting in 2012 to only include a few key variables to ascertain if the study participant has provided care over the past 12 months.
