Component Description

The Chronic Health Conditions (CHRONIC_DER) section includes a variable on self-reported history of any of the following health conditions: coronary heart disease (angina or myocardial infarction), transient ischemic attack, stroke, diabetes, asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (chronic bronchitis or emphysema), arthritis, or cancer.

Analytic Notes

In order to calculate the derived chronic conditions variable, participants needed to complete all relevant questions during Time 1, but the variable was still scored if participants did not complete Time 3: If any variables collected in Time 1 were missing and no other variables had a "Yes" response, then the derived variable was set to missing. If variables collected in Time 3 were missing (cancer, rheumatoid arthritis or osteoarthritis), calculation of the derived variable was based on responses from Time 1 only.

The derived variable in this dataset used variables collected during both the Time 1 and Time 3 portion of the SHOW study and T3 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data. All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3.
