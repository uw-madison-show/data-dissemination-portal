Component Description

The Consent (CON2) form obtains the subjects' consent to participate in various components of the survey that may have differed from previous consents. This codebook is specifically for participants that report wanting to change consent at the time of the blood draw.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18+ are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The Consent Form is first filled out and signed by the subject during the in-home interview. Details convering the intial consent can be found in the main consent codebook (CONS). At the start of the blood draw appointment, participants are asked if any changes in consent have occurred. The CON2 database stores these revised consents. The subject has the right to revoke consent at any point in the survey.

Data Processing

The CON2 section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors by staff at the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

Subjects are allowed to change their consents at any time during the study.
