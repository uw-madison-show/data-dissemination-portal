Component Description

The Consent (CONS) form obtains the subjects' consent to participate in various components of the survey.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The Consent Form is first filled out and signed by the subject during Time 1. Consent can be updated during Time 3 if the subject changes his/her mind. The subject has the right to revoke consent at any point in the survey.

Data Processing

The CONS section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors by staff at the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

Subjects are allowed to change their consents at any time during the study.
