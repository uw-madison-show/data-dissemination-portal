Component Description

The Consent (CONS) form obtains the subjects' consent to participate in various components of the survey.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants 18 years and older are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The Consent Form is filled out and signed by the subject during the in-home interview. The subject has the right to revoke consent at any point in the survey.

Data Processing

The CONS section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors by staff at the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

Subjects are allowed to change their consents at any time during the study. Eight questions in the consent changed from 2013 to 2014, the questions that changed include R2 in the end of their name.
