Component Description

In the Minor Consent (CONS_M) part of the survey the parent’s or guardian’s consent is obtained for the minor to participate in various components of the survey. Additionally, if the minor is 7 years old or older, the minor’s assent is obtained. There are three different consent/assent forms: one that a parent or guardian needs to complete regardless of the age of the minor that is participating, one that minors between 7 and 14 years complete, and one that minors 15 to 17 years old complete.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants 0-17 years old are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The Consent form is completed and signed by the parent/guardian and the subject (if 7 years old or older) during the in-home interview. The parent/guardian/subject has the right to change or revoke consent at any time during the survey.

Data Processing

The CONS_M section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors by staff at the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

In 2014 there were two different versions (V1 and V2) of the assent form were used for 7-14 year olds. Both versions include the same information but in V2 the text in the assent form was made shorter and more appropriate for this particular age group.
