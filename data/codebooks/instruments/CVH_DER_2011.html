Subject: Cardiovascular Health Index

Data File: cvh_der_2011.sas7bdat

Component Description

The cardiovascular health index (modified from Folsom et al 2011) is based on meeting the American Heart Association's recommendations for 7 component of a healthy lifestyle: smoking status, body mass index, physical activity, diet, total cholesterol, blood pressure, and hemoglobin A1c.

Analytic Notes

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study and T3 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data. All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3.

In 2011 there are 147 T3 participants (18%) missing values for the CVH index. As stated above the CVH index is based on 7 components of a healthy lifestyle. If any of the components were missing for a given record then the CVH index was set to missing.

References

Folsom, A. R., Y. Hiroshi, J. A. Nettleton, P. L. Lutsey, M. Cushman, W. D. Rosamond. 2011. Community Prevalence of Ideal Cardiovascular Health, by the AHA definition, and Relation to Cardiovascular Disease Incidence. J. Am. Coll. Cardiol. 57(16): 1690-1696.
