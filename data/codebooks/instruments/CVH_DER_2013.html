Subject: Cardiovascular Health Index

Data File: cvh_der_2013.sas7bdat

Component Description

The cardiovascular health index (modified from Folsom et al 2011) is based on meeting the American Heart Association's recommendation for 7 components of a healthy lifestyle: smoking status, body mass index, physical activity, diet, total cholesterol, blood pressure, and hemoglobin A1c.

Analytic Notes

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study except for the block diet questions (QI) from time 2. Since all but 1 of the components are from time 3, T3 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data. All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3.

In 2013, 8 participants who completed time 3 did not complete the time 2 Self-Administered Questionnaire. As stated above the CVH index is based on 7 components of a healthy lifestyle. If any of the components were missing for a given record then the CVH index was set to missing.

References

Folsom, A. R., Y. Hiroshi, J. A. Nettleton, P. L. Lutsey, M. Cushman, W. D. Rosamond. 2011. Community Prevalence of Ideal Cardiovascular Health, by the AHA definition, and Relation to Cardiovascular Disease Incidence. J. Am. Coll. Cardiol. 57(16): 1690-1696.
