Component Description

The Diabetes (DIAB) section includes variables on the diagnosis, awareness (undiagnosed diabetes), treatment, and control of diabetes. See Analytic Notes for definitions.

Analytic Notes

Diabetes was defined as having a previous physician diagnosis or having HgbA1c >=6.5 at the time of sample collection. Only diagnoses of Type I or Type II (HHQ191) were used; all other types (gestational and borderline diabetes) were not considered as diagnoses of diabetes. Control of diabetes was set to HgbA1c<=7.0.

Treatment for diabetes was identified as use of insulin or oral anti-diabetic agents, as answered in HHQ193_A through HHQ103_F. Variables used in the calculations are from the derived dataset HHQ_DER.

The dataset contains only SPIDs that completed the sample collection (n=412). All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the in-home interview portion of the SHOW study. Analyses interpreted with respect to the target population should incorporate the sampling weights and the complex survey design (see the Analytic Guidelines for more information).

Variable Format FMT_YES_NO
Description Previous diagnosis of Type I or Type II diabetes from physician or HgbA1c >= 6.5
