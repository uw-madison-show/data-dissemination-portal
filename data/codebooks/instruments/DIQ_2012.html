LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Diet (DIQ)

Years of Content: 2012

First Published: May 2013

Last Revised: April 2014

Subject: Diet

Record Source(s): DIQ

Data File: diq_2012.sas7bdat

Component Description

The DIQ is a diet questionnaire that focused on the frequency of certain kinds of foods in people’s diet. 

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The DIQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the exam center portion (Time 3) of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

The DIQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the exam center portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
