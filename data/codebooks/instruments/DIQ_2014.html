Title: Diet (DIQ)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: March 2015

Last Revised: August 2015

Subject: Diet

Record Source(s): DIQ

Data File: diq_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The DIQ is a diet questionnaire that focuses on the frequency of certain types of foods in one's diet.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants aged 18+ are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The DIQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the in home interview of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

The DIQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the CAPI portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the analytical guidelines for information on what weights to use).
