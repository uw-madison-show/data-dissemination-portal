Component Description

The demographics section (DMQ) provides specific information regarding socioeconomic status and racial and ethnic ancestry.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The DMQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the in-home portion (Time 1) of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

The DMQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home portion of the SHOW study (Time 1) and T1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
