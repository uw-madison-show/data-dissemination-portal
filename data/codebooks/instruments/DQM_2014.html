Title: Diet for Minors (DMQ)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: July 2015

Last Revised: August 2015

Subject: Diet

Record Source(s): DQM

Data File: dqm_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The DQM focuses on the frequency of certain types of foods in the minor's diet.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants between 3 and 17 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The DQM section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the In-Home Questionnaire portion of the study.

Data Processing

The DQM section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

Appropriate weights should be used.
