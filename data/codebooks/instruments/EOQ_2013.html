Component Description

The EQ-5D™ is a standardized instrument for use as a measure of health outcome, providing a simple descriptive profile and a single index value for health status. EQ-5D is produced and maintained by the EuroQol Group.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The EOQ section was completed during the first interview of a subject at the subject’s home. It is completed on paper form by the respondent.

Data Processing

EOQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Starting in December 2008 these logical tests were incorporated into the CAPI programming. 

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in home personal interview portion of the SHOW study (Time 1), and T1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
