Component Description

The Framingham Coronary Heart Disease Risk (FHR) section includes variables on the population and individual risk for coronary heart disease (CHD), as estimated from the Framingham Cohort Study.

Analytic Notes

Risk was only estimated for respondents over age 30. Where population or individual risk bounds were calculated (e.g. 10-yr CHD risk >= 0.53), reported relative risks are also bounds as indicated by the variable FHR_NOTE.

Definitions of variables are not exactly as prescribed in the Framingham-based regression. Diabetes was defined as in the DIAB dataset. (See DIAB codebook for details.) Smoking was defined as answering Yes to SMQ040 ("Do you smoke cigarettes now?") or having stopped at the current age, based on SMQ120 ("How old were you when you stopped smoking?") and age at Time 1.

CHD risk was calculated based on score sheets and not regression coefficients. SAS code for related calculations is available upon request.
