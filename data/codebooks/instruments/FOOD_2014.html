Title: Dietary Screener questionnaire

Subject: Food frequency in the past month

Record Source(s): Modified from NHANES Dietary Screener Module DSQ

Data File: food_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

This is modified from NHANES' 26-item Dietary Screener Questionnaire (DSQ), which asks about the frequency of consumption in the past month of selected foods and drinks. This instrument captures intakes of fruits and vegetables, dairy/calcium, added sugars, whole grains/fiber, red meat, and processed meat

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants 18 years and older are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The FOOD section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The FOOD section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the analytical guidelines for information on what weights to use). Souce for questionnaire was the dietary screener questionnaire, with development led by the National Cancer Institute (NCI)

References
