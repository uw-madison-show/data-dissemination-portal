Title: Derived variables - Food

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: June 2015

Last Revised: September 2015

Subject: Food frequency in the past month

Raw Data Source(s): FOOD

Data File: food_der_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

These are the derived variable(s) for the FOOD self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Analytic Notes

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing this data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calculations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data. Additional details are available upon request. Souce for questionnaire was the dietary screener questionnaire, with development led by the National Cancer Institute (NCI)
