Title: Derived Variables - Foreclosures (FOR)

Years of Content: 2013

First Published: May 2014

Last Revised: June 2014

Subject: Foreclosure rates

Raw Data Source(s): Court Data Technologies LLC and United States Census Bureau 2000 and 2010

Data File: for_der_2013.sas7bdat

Component Description

County and census block group foreclosure rates for a participant's survey year and 5 years prior were linked to the SHOW participant by their primary residence.

Analytic Notes

Foreclosure data was purchased from Court Data Technologies LLC, an independent consulting and development firm in Madison, Wisconsin. Census block group and County level household data was downloaded from the United States Census Bureau. Geocoding of foreclosures was processed using Pitney Bowes Inc. CENTRUS software. Foreclosure rates were calculated and linked to SHOW participants using ESRI ArcGIS 10.3. Codebook processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Foreclosure rate values are defined as the number of foreclosures per 1000 households for a given year. Additional details on how all foreclosure variables were calculated is available upon request.
