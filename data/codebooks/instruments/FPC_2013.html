Component Description

The FPC is a questionnaire that asks about contraceptive use, types of contraception used, and reasons for not using contraception. 

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The FPC section was administered via Audio-Computer Assisted Self Interview (ACASI) program during the exam center portion (Time 3) of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

The FPC section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the exam center portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.

FPC010 - PRACTICING CONTRACEPTION

Variable Name FPC010
Variable Format FMT_YES_NO
English Text Some things people do to prevent a pregnancy include abstaining from having sex at certain times, using birth control methods (such as the pill, implants, shots, condoms, diaphragm, foam, and IUD), having their tubes tied, or having a vasectomy. Are you or your partner doing anything now to keep you/her from getting pregnant?
English Instructions If you have more than one partner, please answer these questions about your usual partner.
Target Population Age: 21 – 74
Skip Patterns If "[2] No" -> FPC030_R3 ; If "[.D] Don't know" -> END SURVEY ; If "[.R] Refused" -> END SURVEY
