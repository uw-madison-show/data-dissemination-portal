Component Description

The Food Security (FSQ) section provides specific information relating concerns about having enough food or about the costs of food for the respondent or his/her family.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The FSQ section is completed during the Time 3 (survey center) visit. It is completed via ACASI directly on a laptop by the respondent.

Data Processing

The FSQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of cleaning by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

Questions FSQ151, FSQ170, and FSQ162 were revised in 2011, where use of food assistance programs was asked for the respondent only instead of all members of the household. Responses for these items have been stored as FSQ151R2, FSQ170R2 and FSQ 162 respectively. These data were collected as part of the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
