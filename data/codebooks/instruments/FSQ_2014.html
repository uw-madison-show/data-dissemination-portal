Component Description

The Food Security (FSQ) section provides specific information relating concerns about having enough food or about the costs of food for the respondent or his/her family.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18+ are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The FSQ section is completed during the In-home questionnaire. It is completed via ACASI directly on a laptop by the respondent.

Data Processing

The FSQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of cleaning by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

Questions FSQ151R2, FSQ170R2, and FSQ162R2 were revised in 2011, where use of food assistance programs was asked for the respondent only instead of all members of the household. Questions FSQ002, FSQ003, FSQ004, FSQ010, FSQ011, FSQ012, FSQ013, FSQ014, FSQ020, and FSQ021 were added in 2012. These data were collected as part of the In-Home Questionnaire portion of the SHOW study appropriate sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. Skip patterns changed in 2014 so only respondents that reported issues on FSQ002, FSQ003, or FSQ004 were asked FSQ010 through FSQ021.
