Title: Derived Variables - Geography (GEO_DER)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: July 2015

Last Revised: September 2015

Subject: Geography

Raw Data Source(s): Household sampling frame

Data File: geo_der_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The GEO (Geography) section includes Census geography at the block group level or higher, as well as RUCA, Six State, and Census urban rural classification for each respondent's residence.

Analytic Notes

The use of geographic data below the county level is restricted to manuscripts with official approval from the SHOW manuscript committee and appropriate IRB approval.
