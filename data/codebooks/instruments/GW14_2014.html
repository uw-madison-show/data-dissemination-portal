Component Description

Follow-up questionnaire was sent to those SHOW participants from 2008-2013 who reported 'yes' to having main home water delievered by a private well from SHOW survey HOQ section. The groundwater anncillary study quesitonnaire asks private well owners in SHOW 2008-2013 about their perceptions, behaviors, and use of their private well water. Questions are focused around consumption, testing of contaminants, and water treatment.
