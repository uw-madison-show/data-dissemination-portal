Component Description

The Health and Functioning in Minors (HFM) section asks the respondent about general health, as well as physical and mental conditions. Demographic questions about the highest grade level in school completed and race/ethnicity are also included in this section.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages < 18 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The HFM section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the in-home portion of the SHOW survey.

Data Processing

The HFM section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.Â  Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home questionnaire portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use).
