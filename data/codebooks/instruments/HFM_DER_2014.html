Subject: Health and Functioning

Raw Data Source(s): HFM

Data File: hfm_der_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

These are derived variables for the Health and Functioning in Minors (HFM), administered during the in-home questionnaire portion of the SHOW survey.

Analytic Notes

All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calculations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data. Additional details are available upon request. These data were collected as part of the in-home questionnaire portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use).
