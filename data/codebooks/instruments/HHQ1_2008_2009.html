Data File: hhq1_2008_2009.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Health History 1 (HHQ1) section provides specific information relating to the respondent’s diagnosed health conditions and medical treatment.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 were eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The HHQ1 section was completed during the in-home (Time1) interview of a participant by a trained interviewer using paper-assisted personal interview (PAPI) from June 2008-November 2008, and computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) starting in December 2008.

Data Processing

The HHQ1 section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. Starting in December 2008 these logical tests were incorporated into the CAPI programming. Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home personal interview (Time 1) portion of the SHOW study. Time 1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
Some variables are not included in the data set. These include variables that denote whether or not the questionnaire was completed and time to completion of questionnaire. These variables are available upon request. Codes for answer options in HHQ217 and HHQ218 have been changed in 2010 (Answer options themselves have not been changed). In order to have consistency across the year, code of 2008-2009 has been changed as code of 2010.
