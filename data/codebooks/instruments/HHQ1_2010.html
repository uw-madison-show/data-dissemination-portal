LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Health History 1 (HHQ1)

Years of Content: 2010

First Published: April 2011

Last Revised: April 2013

Subject: History of Health conditions

Record Source(s): SHOW original questions and some modified from NHANES.

Data File: hhq1_2010.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Health History 1 (HHQ1) section provides specific information relating to the respondent’s diagnosed health conditions and medical treatment.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 were eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The HHQ1 section was completed during the in-home (Time1) interview of a participant by a trained interviewer using computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) form January 2010 to December 2010.

Data Processing

The HHQ1 section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home personal interview (Time 1) portion of the SHOW study. Time 1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
Some variables are not included in the data set. These include variables that denote whether or not the questionnaire was completed and time to completion of questionnaire. These variables are available upon request. Codes for answer options in HHQ217 and HHQ218 have been changed at some point in 2010 (Options themselves have not been changed). Cleaning was done as much as possible to be consistent across the year, but there is still a possibility of misclassification. HHQ110,HHQ112, HHQ120, HHQ122, HHQ131, HHQ150, HHQ151, HHQ152, HHQ161, HHQ170, HHQ172, HHQ220 HHQ250, HHQ252, HHQ280, HHQ281_A---HHQ281_OTHER, HHQ284_1---HHQ284_OTHER, HHQ290, HHQ291, HHQ300, HHQ301_A ---HHQ301_OTHER, HHQ310, HHQ311_A ---HHQ311_OTHER, HHQ312_1 ---HHQ312_16, HHQ320, and HHQ321_ A ---HHQ321_OTHER were dropped in 2010. HHQ130, HHQ160, HHQ162, HHQ230 were slightly modified and variable names were changed as HHQ130_R2, HHQ160_R2, HHQ162_R2, and HHQ230_R2.
