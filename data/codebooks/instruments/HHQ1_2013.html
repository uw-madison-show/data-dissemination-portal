LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Health History 1 (HHQ1)

Years of Content: 2013

First Published: April 2014

Last Revised: June 2015

Subject: History of Health conditions

Record Source(s): SHOW original questions and some modified from NHANES

Data File: hhq1_2013.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Health History 1 (HHQ1) section provides specific information relating to the respondent’s diagnosed health conditions and medical treatment.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 were eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The HHQ1 section was completed during the in-home (Time1) interview of a participant by a trained interviewer using computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) form January 2013 to December 2013.

Data Processing

The HHQ1 section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home personal interview (Time 1) portion of the SHOW study. Time 1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. Some variables are not included in the data set. These include variables that denote whether or not the questionnaire was completed and time to completion of questionnaire. These variables are available upon request.
