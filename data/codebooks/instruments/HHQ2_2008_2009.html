Component Description

The Health History 2 (HHQ2) section provides specific information relating to the respondent’s diagnosed health conditions, medical treatment, and family history of disease.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 were eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The HHQ2 section was completed during the survey center (Time 3) visit. The participant was interviewed by a trained interviewer using paper-assisted personal interview (PAPI) from June 2008-November 2008, and computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) starting in December 2008.

Data Processing

The HHQ2 section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. Starting in December 2008 these logical tests were incorporated into the CAPI programming. Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study. Time 3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
Some variables are not included in the data set. These include variables that denote whether or not the questionnaire was completed and time to completion of questionnaire. These variables are available upon request.
