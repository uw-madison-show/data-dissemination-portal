Subject: Individual and family history of health conditions

Raw Data Source(s): HHQ2

Data File: hhq2_der_2011.sas7bdat

Component Description

These are the derived variables for the Health History Questionnaire - part 2, administered during the Time 3 (survey center) portion of the SHOW survey.

Analytic Notes

Because the HHQ2 Questionnaire does not allow for participants to respond that they have never had a chronic disease in response to HHQ580, and the question is a "Mark all that apply," if the respondent answered the previous (HHQ570), the response to HHQ580 is coded as "2: No" rather than missing.

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study and T3 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data. All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calculations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data. Additional details are available upon request.
