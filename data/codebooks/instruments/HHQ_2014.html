LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Health History (HHQ)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: May 2015

Last Revised: July 2015

Subject: Individual and family history of health conditions

Record Source(s): SHOW original questions and some modified from NHANES

Data File: hhq_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Health History (HHQ) section provides specific information relating to the respondent’s diagnosed health conditions, medical treatment, and family history of disease.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18+ were eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The HHQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program form July 2014 to January 2015.

Data Processing

The HHQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the CAPI portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). This questionnaire includes questions that were contained in the HHQ1, HHQ2, SIQ, SDQ, and CGQ questionnaires in prior years. Some questions are similar to, but slightly different from, questions from prior years. Due to an error in skip pattern programming, only participants who answered Yes to HHQ140 were asked HHQ150. All other participants skipped to HHQ160.
