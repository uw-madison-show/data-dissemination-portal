LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Derived Variables - Health History (HHQ_DER)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: May 2015

Last Revised: July 2015

Subject: Individual and family history of health conditions

Raw Data Source(s): HHQ

Data File: hhq_der_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

These are the derived variables for the Health History Questionnaire, administered during the CAPI-administered portion of the SHOW survey.

Analytic Notes

Because the HHQ Questionnaire does not allow for participants to respond that they have never had a chronic disease in response to HHQ580, and the question is a "Mark all that apply," if the respondent answered the previous (HHQ570), the response to HHQ580 is coded as "2: No" rather than missing.

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the CAPI portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the 2014 Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calculations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data.
