Years of Content: 2011

First Published: April 2012

Last Revised: May 2012

Subject: Housing Characteristics

Record Source(s): Modified from the Housing Characteristics Questionnaire in NHANES

Data File: hoq_2011.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Housing Characteristics questionnaire provides specific information relating to the physical home environment.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

Information about the dwelling characteristics was obtained by trained interviewers during the household interview by computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI).

Data Processing

The Housing Characteristics questionnaire data were reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during data collection. The CAPI system is programmed with automated data consistency checks to reduce data entry errors. 

Analytic Notes

As with 2010, a new option for "Other" was made available as a response for main type of heating system. Passive voice constructions for HOQ260 and HOQ265 were used only on the backup paper versions and not routinely administered. New variables were introduced for pet ownership questions, allowing multiple "other" responses. This data is available for use but has not been processed and is not included in the dataset.
