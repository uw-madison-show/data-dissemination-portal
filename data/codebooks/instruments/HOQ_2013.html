Component Description

The Housing Characteristics (HOQ) questionnaire provides specific information relating to the physical home environment.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The HOQ section was completed during the in-home (Time1) interview of a participant by a trained interviewer using computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) from January 2013 to December 2013.

Data Processing

The Housing Characteristics questionnaire data were reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. The CAPI system is programmed with automated data consistency checks to reduce data entry errors.Â Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home personal interview (Time 1) portion of the SHOW study. Time 1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. Some variables are not included in the data set. Data regarding number of "Other" pet ownership is available for use, but has not been processed and is not included in the dataset. HOQ065_R2 HOQ065_10 and HOQ065_20 were added in 2013. They were derived from Census questions to ascertain home ownership and multi-unit housing.
