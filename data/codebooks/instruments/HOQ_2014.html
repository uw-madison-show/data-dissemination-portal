Component Description

The Housing Characteristics (HOQ) questionnaire provides specific information relating to the physical home environment.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants age 18+ are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The HOQ section was included in a a booklet of self-administered questionniares provided to study participants during the in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The Housing Characteristics questionnaire data were reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. Â Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the 2014 Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). The questionniare changed mode this year. The HOQ section was completed during the in-home (Time1) interview of a participant by a trained interviewer using computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) from January 2013 to December 2013. This year the HOQ section was included in a a booklet of self-administered questionniares provided to study participants during the in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.
