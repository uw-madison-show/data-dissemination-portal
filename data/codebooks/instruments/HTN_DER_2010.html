Component Description

The Hypertension (HTN) section includes variables on the diagnosis, awareness (undiagnosed hypertension), treatment, and control of hypertension. See Analytic Notes for definitions.

Analytic Notes

Hypertension was defined as self-reported current use of anti-hypertensive medication or having systolic blood pressure >= 140 mm Hg or diastolic blood pressure >= 90 mm Hg at the Time 3 measurement. Awareness and treatment of hypertension were defined for hypertensive participants based on the previous definition. Awareness of hypertensive status was based on a self report of ever being diagnosed with hypertension (see HHQ200). Treatment of hypertension was defined as current use of anti-hypertensive medication (see HHQ203_2). Control of hypertension among participants currently taking anti-hypertensive medication was defined as systolic blood pressure < 140 mm Hg and diastolic blood pressure < 90 mm Hg. The dataset contains only SPIDs who completed Time 3 (N=862). 7 SPIDs are missing values for hypertension; 6 of these SPIDs are missing Time 3 blood pressure measurements and the other SPID is missing self-reported current use of anti-hypertensive medication.

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study and T3 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data.

HTN14090 - HYPERTENSION 140/90 OR CURRENT ANTI-HYPERTENSIVE MEDICATION

Variable Name HTN14090
Variable Format FMT_YES_NO
Description Current prescription for antihypertensives or systolic BP>=140 or diastolic BP>=90.
Calculation HTN14090=.; IF BP_SYSTOLIC_23>0 AND BP_DIASTOLIC_23>=0 THEN DO; IF HHQ203_2=1 or BP_SYSTOLIC_23>=140 or BP_DIASTOLIC_23>=90 THEN HTN14090=1; IF HHQ203_2=2 and 0<BP_SYSTOLIC_23<140 and 0<=BP_DIASTOLIC_23<90 THEN HTN14090=2; END;
