Record Source: SHOW original questions and some modified from NHANES and NHIS

Data File: iuq_2008_2009.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Insurance, Access, and Utilization section (IUQ) provides specific information relating to health insurance and the respondents use of the health care system.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The IUQ section was completed during the first interview of a subject at the subject’s home by a trained interviewer using PAPI from June 2008-November 2008 and using CAPI starting in December 2008.

Data Processing and Editing

IUQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Starting in December 2008 these logical tests were incorporated into the CAPI programming.

Known Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in home personal interview portion of the SHOW study (Time 1), and T1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
