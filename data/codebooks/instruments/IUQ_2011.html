Data File: iuq_2011.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Insurance, Access, and Utilization section (IUQ) provides specific information relating to health insurance and the respondents use of the health care system.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The IUQ section was completed during the in-home (Time1) interview of a participant by a trained interviewer using computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) form January 2011 to December 2011.

Data Processing

IUQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home personal interview portion of the SHOW study (Time 1), and T1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. There are some data issues in IUQ020. The answer options for IUQ020, were newly modifed in 2011 (<7> "General Assitance Medical Program(GAMP)" was removed, "Medicare part D" in <4> was moved to <11> as "Medicare Par D/Seniorcare prescription drug coverage"). However, old version of handcard was still used during interview in 2011.
