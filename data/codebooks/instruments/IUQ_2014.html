Data File: iuq_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Insurance, Access, and Utilization (IUQ) section provides specific information relating to health insurance and use of the health care system.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18 years and older are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The IUQ section was completed during the in-home interview and was administered by a trained interviewer using computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) between June 2014 and February 2015.

Data Processing

IUQ data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

Analyses interpreted with respect to the target population should incorporate the sampling weights and the complex survey design (see the Analytic Guidelines for more information).

Questions: IUQ012, IUQ013, IUQ014, IUQ015, IUQ021, IUQ023, IUQ025, IUQ026, IUQ027 were newly added in 2014. Question IUQ020 (now IUQ020_R2) and it's response options changed slightly from 2013 and the answer options for IUQ265 (now IUQ260_R2) did change.
