Title: Key (KEY)

Years of Content: 2010

First Published: April 2011

Last Revised: May 2012

Subject: Key variables required for almost all analyses

Recored Source(s): Household screener modified from NHANES.

Data File: key_2010.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Key (KEY) section provides details about survey participant’s age and gender, household enumeration by age and gender, household geography, and sampling design and weights.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The KEY section contains variables collected by means of an in-person household screener. The contact person for the household screener must be at least 18 years old and knowledgable about the household residents.

Data Processing

The KEY section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

See section titled Analytic Guidelines for more information on SHOW’s sampling design, weights, and variance estimation.
