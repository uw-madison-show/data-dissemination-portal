Title: Key (KEY)

Years of Content: 2013

First Published: May 2014

Subject: Key variables required for almost all analyses

Record Source(s): Household screener modified from NHANES

Data File: key_2013.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Key (KEY) section includes the survey participant’s age, gender, year of annual sample, and household identification number.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The KEY section contains variables collected by means of an in-person household screening and verification of age questionnaire conducted by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the in-home portion of the SHOW

Data Processing

The KEY section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

Age at consent was calculated as the number of days from the respondent's date of birth to the date of informed consent divided by 365.25. This calculation used the best available date of birth data.
