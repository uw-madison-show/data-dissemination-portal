Subject: Biological sample collection and testing results

Record Source(s):

Data File: lab_2012.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Laboratory (LAB) section provides details about the sample collection process and results from laboratory testing on specimens collected.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The LAB section is administered during the Exam Center visit in person with results recorded on paper and then entered into a database after review.

Data Processing

This section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by SHOW staff. Values obtained from Marshfield Clinic have undergone their interal quality assurance/quality control processes.

Analytic Notes

The variable LAB241 was implemented this year to record the time saliva specimens were frozen. Draw restriction (LAB010) were handled differently than is previous cohorts; they were administered as a series of yes/no questions. Raw data for these answers is available in the derived dataset only. Saliva was only collected on respondents who either failed or refused the blood draw.

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
