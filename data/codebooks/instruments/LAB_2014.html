Subject: Biological sample collection and testing results

Data File: lab_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Laboratory (LAB) section provides details about the sample collection process and results from laboratory testing on specimens collected.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18+ are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The LAB section is administered during the sample collection visit in person with results recorded on paper and then entered into a database after review.

Data Processing

This section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by SHOW staff. Values obtained from Marshfield Clinic have undergone their interal quality assurance/quality control processes.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of thesample portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use).
