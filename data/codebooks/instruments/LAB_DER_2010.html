Years of Content: 2010

First Published: May 2012

Last Revised: May 2012

Subject: Biological sample collection and testing results

Raw Data Source(s): LAB

Data File: lab_der_2010.sas7bdat

Component Description

These are the derived variables for the laboratory module administered during the Time 3 (survey center) portion of the SHOW survey.

Analytic Notes

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study and T3 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data. All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calcul ations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data. Additional details are available upon request.
