LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Characteristics of Your Neighborhood (NBRHD)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: July 2015

Last Revised: July 2015

Subject: Neighborhood characteristics

Record Source(s): Modified from the Neighborhood Environment Walkability Scale (NEWS)

Data File: nbrhd_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Characteristics of your Neighborhood section (NBRHD) provides information on walking distances from participant's home to specific destionations, subjective evaluations of the participant's neighborhood, and the importance of factors that may have influenced the participant's decion on moving to current residence (new this year).

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18 years and older are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The NBRHD section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The NBRHD section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study and appropriate weights should be used when analysing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). This year, the answer option from previous years "0-10 minutes" is now "0-5 minutes" and "6-10 minutes". In question 1 instead of asking about walking distance to a public indoor and outdoor swimming pool (two questions), now it is only asked about walking distance to a public swimming pool. Question about if your neighborhood is free from garbage was dropped. Question 6 (6a-6p) was added this year.
