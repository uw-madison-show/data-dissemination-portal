Data File: ocq_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The occupation (OCQ) section asks questions about the respondent’s work experience. This section goes into details on the type of work that was done and duties performed so that this information can be accurately categorized into occupations.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18+ are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The OCQ section was completed during the in-home interview of a subject by a trained interviewer using computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI). Interviewers used active probes to get a clear picture of what they did on this job so trained coders can assign "standard industry and occupation codes".

Data Processing

OCQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home personal interview portion of the SHOW study. See the analytical guidelines for appropriate sample weights when analyzing these data. Questions about the longest held job were dropped from the 2014 survey.
