Component Description

The OHQ section provides information relating to individual’s basic oral health by doing oral screen examination.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The OHQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the exam center portion (Time 3) of the SHOW study.

Data Processing and Editing

The OHQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

known Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the exam center portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.

OHQ section is only in 2010 dataset.

OHQ section was implemented after early June in 2010, so OHQ section was not done in some part of 2010 dataset.
