Component Description

The Physical Activity for Minors (PAM) section asks the respondent about physical activites that are done over a typical week or typical day.  The first section of questions are asked of those age >=12 and <18 and the second section of questions are asked of those age >=3 and <12. The sections contain physcial activity quetions pertaining to general physical acitivity followed by questions pertaining to physical activity at school.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages >=3 and < 18 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The PAM section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program.

Data Processing

The PAM section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the CAPI portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). PAM010 may be found in the KEY for Minors codebook.
