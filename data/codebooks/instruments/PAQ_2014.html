Component Description

The Physical Activity and Physical Fitness (PAQ) section asks the respondent about physical activites that are done over a typical week or typical day.  The first series of questions are related to work, then transportation, followed by questions regarding physical activities during recreational time. The last series of questions are related to activities that involve sitting and most questions in this series are asked about the last 30 days.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18+ are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The PAQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program.

Data Processing

The PAQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the CAPI portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). Some questions are similar to, but slightly different from, prior years PAQ questions.
