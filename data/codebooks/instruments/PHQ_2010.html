Title: Depression Diagnostic and Severity Measure (PHQ-8)

Years of Content: 2010

First Published: February 2011

Last Revised: February 2011

Access and Use Constraints: None

Subject: Depression

Record Source: PHQ-8

Data File: phq_2010.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Depression diagnostic and severity measure uses the standard PHQ-8 instrument based on DSM-IV diagnosis of depression.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The PHQ-8 was approved for inclusion on April 13, 2010. It was initially conducted on paper until xxxdatexxx. Subsequently, it was administered through CAPI. From xxxdatexxx it was administered through ACASI.

Data Processing and Editing

PHQ-8 data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  

Known Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the exam center portion of the SHOW study (Time 3), and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.

References

Kroenke K, Spitzer RL. The PHQ-9: a new depression and diagnostic severity measure, Psychiatric Ann. 2002; 32: 509–521.

Kroenke K, Strine TW, Spitzer RL, Williams JB, Berry JT, Mokdad AH. The PHQ-8 as a measure of current depression in the general population. J Affect Disord. 2009 Apr;114(1-3):163-73.
