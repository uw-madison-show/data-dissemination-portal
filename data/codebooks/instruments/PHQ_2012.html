Subject: Depression

Component Description

The Depression diagnostic and severity measure uses the standard PHQ-8 instrument based on DSM-IV diagnosis of depression.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The PHQ-8 was administered via Audio-Computer Assisted Self Interview (ACASI) program during the exam center portion (Time 3) of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

PHQ-8 data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

When the instrument was administered, responses were coded as [1] NOT AT ALL, [2] SEVERAL DAYS, [3] MORE THAN HALF THE DAYS, [4] NEARLY EVERY DAY. These were recoded to 0-3 to match standard PHQ-8 administration. These data were collected as part of the exam center portion of the SHOW study (Time 3), and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
