Data File: phq_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The PHQ-2 inquires about the frequency of depressed mood and anhedonia over the past two weeks. The PHQ-2 includes the first two items of the PHQ-9.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18 and older are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The PHQ-2 was administered via Audio-Computer Assisted Self Interview (ACASI) program during the in-home portion of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

PHQ-2 data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

When the instrument was administered, responses were coded as [1] NOT AT ALL, [2] SEVERAL DAYS, [3] MORE THAN HALF THE DAYS, [4] NEARLY EVERY DAY. These were recoded to 0-3 to match standard PHQ-2 administration.

Sampling weights should be used when analyzing these data (See 2014 analytic guidelines for more information on which weights to use).

References

Kroenke K, Spitzer RL, Williams JB. The Patient Health Questionniare-2: Validity of a Two-item Depression Screener. Medical Care 2003, (41) 1284-1294.
