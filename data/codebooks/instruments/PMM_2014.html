Data File: pmm_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The PMM provides information on physical measures (height and weight), measures of blood pressure, and spirometry testing measured as Forced Vital Capacity (FVC) and Forced Expiratory Volume in 1 second (FEV1).

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 3-17 years old are eligible for this section. Height and weight is measured for participants >= 3 years. Blood pressure is measured for participants >=6 years. Spirometry testing is only done on participants who had been identified as eligible for spirometry testing according to questions in Respiratory Health in Minors (RHM) and are >= 6 years old.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The PMM section was completed during the in-home interview and was administered by a trained interviewer using computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) between June 2014 and February 2015.

Data Processing

This section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Standing height was measured two times, if the two values were not within 2 inches of each other, the instrument prompted the interviewer to measure height for the third time. Numerical values for measured height were typed twice during data entry to reduce clerical errors. FVC and FEV1 values were estimated three times, if two of the FEV1 values were not within 10% of the highest value the instrument prompted the interviewer for additional measures (up to eight measures).

Analytic Notes

Analyses interpreted with respect to the target population should incorporate the sampling weights and the complex survey design (see the Analytic Guidelines for more information).
