Component Description

These are the derived variables for the Physical Measurments in Minors (PMM) module that covers measured and self-reported height and weight, blood pressure measures, and measures of lung function (FEV1 & FVC).

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home minor portion of the SHOW study and appropriate weights should be used when analysing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use).

All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calculations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data. Additional details are available upon request.

For the spirometry testing (FEV1 & FVC) observations are considered valid if 2 readings are within 10% of the maximum reading. Population-predicted values for respondents who reported Hispanic ethnicity were derived from estimates from the Mexican-American population; no population-predicted values were calculated for respondents who indicated a race of "Other". Normal population is determined based on self-report of ever diagnosis of asthma (RHM030)

References

Hankinson JL, Odencrantz JR, Fedan KB (1999). Spirometric Reference Values from a Sample of the General U.S. Population. Am J Respir Crit Care Med 159(1), 179-187.
