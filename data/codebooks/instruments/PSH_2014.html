Years of Content: 2014

First Published: May 2015

Last Revised: July 2015

Subject: Screening, Prevention, and Safety Habits

Record Source(s): Modified from the National Health Survey Series, National Longitudinal Study of Adolescent Health

Data File: psh_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The PSH section provides specific information relating to common screening tests and vaccinations that have been recommended for different age groups by different health organizations. It also provides specific information relating to an individual's prevention and safety habits.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18+ were eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The PSH section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The PSH section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (SAQ) and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see theAnalytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). This questionnaire includes questions that were contained in the QB and SIQ questionnaires in prior years. SIQ questions were previously administered by computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the in-home interview and have changed mode in 2014.
