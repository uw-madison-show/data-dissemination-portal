Data File: ptsd_2013.sas7bdat

Component Description

PTSD CheckList - Civilian Version for DSM_IV is a standardized self-report rating scale for PTSD comprising 17 items that correspond to the key symptoms of PTSD. PTSD CheckList - Civilian Version for DSM_IV is applied generally to any traumatic event.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The PTSD section was administered via Audio-Computer Assisted Self Interview (ACASI) program during the exam center portion (Time 3) of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

The PTSD section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin. Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the exam center portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.

References

Weathers, F.W., Huska, J.A., Keane, T.M. PCL-C for DSM-IV. Boston: National Center for PTSD – Behavioral Science Division, 1991.
