Component Description

This instrument included the 6 item Abbreviated PCL-C (items 1, 4, 7, 10, 14, and 15), which is a shortened version of the PTSD Checklist â€“ Civilian version (PCL-C), as well as item 13 from PCL-C.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18 and older are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The PTSD section was administered as a Self-Administerd Questionnaire (SAQ).

Data Processing

The PTSD section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin. Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

Sampling weights should be used when analyzing these data (See the 2014 anaytic guidelines for more information about which weights to use).
