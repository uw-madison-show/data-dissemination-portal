Years of Content: 2010

Last Revised: May 2013

Subject: 17 items that correspond to the key symptoms of PTSD

Raw Data Source(s): PTSD

Data File: ptsd_der_2010.sas7bdat

Component Description

These are the derived variables for PTSD CheckList – Civilian Version for DSM_IV.

Analytic Notes

Scores and related statistics were calculated for all respondents who answered all 17 items that correspond to the key symptoms of PTSD. All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 3 portion of the SHOW st udy and T3 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data. All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calculations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data. Additional details are av ailable upon request.

References

Weathers, F.W., Huska, J.A., Keane, T.M. PCL-C for DSM-IV. Boston: National Center for PTSD – Behavioral Science Division, 1991.
