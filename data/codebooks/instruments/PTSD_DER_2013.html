Component Description

These are the derived variables for PTSD CheckList – Civilian Version for DSM_IV.

Analytic Notes

Scores and related statistics were calculated for all respondents who answered all 17 items that correspond to the key symptoms of PTSD. Respondents who omitted answers may still have PTSD based on completed responses even though no derived variables are included. The cut-point for diagnosis by score is 50 in this dataset; literature suggests that this provides high specificity but low sensitivity and that the cut-point used in research may depend on the application.

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study and T3 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data. All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calculations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data. Additional details are available upon request.

DX_PTSD_DSMIV - DIAGNOSIS OF PTSD MADE BY DSM-IV SYMPTOM CRITERIA

Variable Name DX_PTSD_DSMIV
Variable Format FMT_YES_NO
Description Diagnosis of PTSD made by DSM-IV symptom criteria, defined by symptoms rated "Moderately" or above on at least 1 B item (questions 1-5), 3 C items (questions 6-12), and at least 2 D items (questions 13-17)
Calculation IF PTSD_TOTAL_MISSING=0 THEN DO;
    ARRAY B PTSD01-PTSD05; COUNT_B=0;
    ARRAY C PTSD06-PTSD12; COUNT_C=0;
    ARRAY D PTSD13-PTSD17; COUNT_D=0;
    DO OVER B;
        COUNT_B=COUNT_B+(B>=3);
    END;
    DO OVER C;
        COUNT_C=COUNT_C+(C>=3);
    END;
    DO OVER D;
        COUNT_D=COUNT_D+(D>=3);
    END;
    IF COUNT_B>=1 AND COUNT_C>=3 AND COUNT_D>=2 THEN DX_PTSD_DSMIV=1;
    ELSE DX_PTSD_DSMIV=2;
END;
Target Population Age: 21 – 74 and not missing any responses for PTSD01-PTSD17
