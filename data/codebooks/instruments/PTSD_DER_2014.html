Analytic Notes

Scores and related statistics were calculated for all respondents who answered all 6 items that correspond to the key symptoms of PTSD. Respondents who omitted answers may still have PTSD based on completed responses even though no derived variables are included. The cut-point for diagnosis by score is 14 in this dataset.

Sampling weights should be used when analyzing these data (See the 2014 anaytic guidelines for more information about which weights to use). All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calculations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data. Additional details are available upon request.
