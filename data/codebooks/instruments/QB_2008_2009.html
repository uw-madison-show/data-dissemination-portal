Record Source: Modified from National Health Survey Series, National Longitudinal Study of

                          Adolecent Health and BRFSS

Data File: qb_2008_2009.sas7bdat

Component Description

The QB section provides specific information relating to individual’s prevention and safety habits.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The QB section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the first in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing and Editing

The QB section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Known Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (Time 2) and T2 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
