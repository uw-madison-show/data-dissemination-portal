Years of Content: 2013

Last Revised: April 2014

Subject: Prevention and Safety Habits

Record Source(s): Modified from the National Health Survey Series, National Longitudinal Study of Adolescent Health

Data File: qb_2013.sas7bdat

Component Description

The QB section provides specific information relating to individual's prevention and safety habits.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The QB section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the first in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The QB section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (Time 2) and T2 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. In QB_PHS_4A, QB_PHS_5A, and QB_PHS_5B, numeric answer option include "Don't Know." In this case, numeric value was changed into .D (Don't know).
