Component Description

These are the derived variables for the Life Events (QC) module included in the Time 2 (self-administered questionnaire) portion of the SHOW survey.

Analytic Notes

In our questionnaire, there are additional two questions (QC_LIFE_44: None of these events happened in the past 12 months, QC_LIFE_45: Don't know) at the end of questions of 43 life events. If there were inconsistent findings between 43 life event questions and those two variables (e.g. marked "some events" among 43 questions AND marked "nothing happened" as well), we disregarded the information from last two questions. If there was any missing value among 43 questions, SRRS score was treated as "missing". All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 2 portion of the SHOW study and T2 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data. All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calculations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data. Additional details are available upon request.
