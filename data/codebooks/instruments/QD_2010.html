LOCATOR RECORD:

Title: Section D: Sleep Habits and Problems

Years of Content: 2010

First Published: February 2011

Last Revised: N/A

Access and Use Constraints: None

Subject: Sleep habits, Sleep disorders and Excessive daytime sleepiness

Record Source: Modified from NHANES Sleep questionnaire, Epworth Sleepiness questionnaire and other sources.

Data File: QD_2010.sas7bdat

Component Description

The QD_SLEEP section provides specific information relating to individuals’ sleep patterns and disorders related to sleep.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 were eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The QD_SLEEP section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the first in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing and Editing

The QD_SLEEP section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (Time 2) and T2 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
