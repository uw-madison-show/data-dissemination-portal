Title: Stress (QE)

Years of Content: 2011

First Published: March 2012

Last Revised: May 2012

Subject: Stress, Anxiety, and Depression

Record Source(s): Jackson Heart Study (Stress, STS); DASS

Data File: qe_2011.sas7bdat

Component Description

The QE_STRESS section measures how often people have feelings and behaviors that might indicate stress, anxiety, and depression.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The QE_STRESS section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the first in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The QE_STRESS section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (Time 2) and T2 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.

References

Lovibond, S.H. & Lovibond, P.F. (1995).  Manual for the Depression Anxiety Stress Scales. (2nd. Ed.)  Sydney: Psychology Foundation. Note: Not scaled for missing answers.
