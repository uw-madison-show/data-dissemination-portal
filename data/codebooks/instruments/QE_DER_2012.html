Subject: Stress, Anxiety, and Depression

Raw Data Source(s): QE

Data File: qe_der_2012.sas7bdat

Component Description

These are the derived variables for the Stress (QE) module included in the Time 2 (self-administered questionnaire) portion of the SHOW survey.

Analytic Notes

Scores and related statistics were calculated for all respondents who answered at least 7 of the 14 questions for each of the stress, anxiety, and depression concepts. Interpretation of values obtained from incomplete surveys should be taken in the context of these missing values. Z scores were calculated using mean and standard error from the validation study referenced below and not from the SHOW population.

References

Lovibond, S.H. & Lovibond, P.F. (1995). Manual for the Depression Anxiety Stress Scales. (2nd. Ed.) Sydney: Psychology Foundation.
