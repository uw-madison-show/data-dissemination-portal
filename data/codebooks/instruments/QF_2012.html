LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Military Experience (QF)

Years of Content: 2012

First Published: April 2013

Last Revised: May 2013

Subject: Military Experience

Record Source(s): Modified from the 2001 National Survey of Veterans

Data File: qf_2012.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Military Experience section (QF_MIL) provides specific information relating to military service in the United States Armed Forces, either in the regular military or in the National Guard or Reserve unit.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The QF_MIL section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the first in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The QF_MIL section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (Time 2) and T2 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
