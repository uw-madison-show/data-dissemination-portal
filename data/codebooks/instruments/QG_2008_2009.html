Component Description

The Discrimination section (QG_DSC) provides specific information relating to how the respondent have been treated during his/her life.  Answers to questions regarding unfair treatment related to race, age, gender, culture, relition, physical appearance, and secual orientation are recorded.  

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The QF_DSC section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the first in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing and Editing

The QF_DSC section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Known Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (Time 2) and T2 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
