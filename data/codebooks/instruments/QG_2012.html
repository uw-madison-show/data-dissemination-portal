Component Description

The Discrimination section (QG_DSC) provides specific information relating to how the respondent has been treated during his/her life.  Answers to questions regarding unfair treatment related to race, age, gender, culture, religion, physical appearance, and sexual orientation are recorded.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The QG_DSC section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the first in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The QG_DSC section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by SHOW staff.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (Time 2) and T2 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.

References

Williams, David R., et al. "Racial Differences in Physical and Mental Health Socio-economic Status, Stress and Discrimination." Journal of Health Psychology 2.3 (1997): 335-351.
