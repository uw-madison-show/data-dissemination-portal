LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Section H: Characteristics of your Neighborhood

Years of Content: 2008-2009

First Published: February 2011

Last Revised: June 2011

Access and Use Constraints: None

Subject: Neighborhood characteristics

Record Source: Modified from the Neighborhood Environment Walkability Scale (NEWS)

Data File: qh_2008_2009.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Characteristics of your Neighborhood section (QH_NBRHD) provides specific information on walking distances to destionations and subjective evaluations of the neighborhood.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The QH_NBRHD section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the first in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing and Editing

The QH_NBRHD section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Known Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (Time 2) and T2 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
