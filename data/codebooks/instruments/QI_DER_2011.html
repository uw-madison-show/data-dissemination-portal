Component Description

These are the derived variables for Section I (Foods You Eat) during self-administered Time 2 portion of the SHOW survey. It was modified from the Block Dietary Data Systems (BDDS) questionnaires created by NutritionQuest. The derived variables were created by NutritionQuest.

Analytic Notes

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 2 portion of the SHOW study and T2 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data. All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Calculations details are not available as NutritionQuest uses a proprietary method to derive the results.

Values supplied for daily totals of calories, protein, fat, carbohydrate, and dietary fiber may be less reliable and should be used with caution since they are not the primary diet analyses for this survey.
