Component Description

The Respiratory Health for Minors (RHM) section provides specific information on minors' athma history and treatment and eligibility to complete spirometry.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 0 - 17 are eligible for this section. This section is completed by an adult proxy.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The RHM section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program from July 2014 to January 2015.

Data Processing

The RHM section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the CAPI portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the 2014 Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). This questionnaire is new in 2014.
