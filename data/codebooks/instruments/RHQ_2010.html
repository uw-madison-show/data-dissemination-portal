Subject: Female Reproductive Health

Record Source(s): Modified from NHANES Reproductive Health Questionnaire

Data File: rhq_2010.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Reproductive Health (RHQ) section provides specific information relating to female reproductive health including questions about hormone use, pregnancy, and maternity.

Eligible Sample

SHOW female participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The RHQ section is completed during the Time 3 (survey center) visit. It is primarily completed via computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI)

Data Processing

The RHQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of cleaning by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. Some variables are not included in the data set. These include variables that denote whether or not th e questionnaire was completed and time to completion of questionnaire. These variables are available upon request. No respondents answered length of breastfeeding for more than six children, so all variables after “6” are removed from RHQ221. No re spondents gave more than six reasons for not starting/stopping breastfeeding, so all variables after “F” are removed from RHQ225. Responses to RHQ225 of “other(specify)” were combined per SP for ease of use. Bracketed text is an “other” response fo r an individual child. These responses include text entered by the respondent as a reason for stopping or not starting breastfeeding.
