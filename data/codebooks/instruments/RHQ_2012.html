Subject: Female Reproductive Health

Record Source(s): RHQ

Data File: rhq_2012.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Reproductive Health (RHQ) section provides specific information relating to female reproductive health including questions about hormone use, pregnancy, and maternity.

Eligible Sample

SHOW female participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The RHQ section is completed during the Time 3 (survey center) visit. It is primarily completed via computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI)

Data Processing

The RHQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of cleaning by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

RHQ221 was modified beginning in 2012 to include the answer, "Did not breasfeed."

These data were collected as part of the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. Some variables are not included in the data set. These include variables that denote whether or not the questionnaire was completed and time to completion of questionnaire. These variables are available upon request.
