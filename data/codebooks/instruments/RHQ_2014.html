Record Source(s): RHQ

Data File: rhq_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Reproductive Health (RHQ) section provides specific information about contraceptive use, types of contraception used, and reasons for not using contraception.  Women are also asked about reproductive health including questions about common contraceptive use, screening tests, hormone use, pregnancy, and maternity.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18+ were eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The RHQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program from July 2014 to January 2015.

Data Processing

The RHQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the CAPI portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the 2014 Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). This questionnaire includes questions that were contained in the RHQ, FPC, and SIQ questionnaires in prior years. In previous years, the FPC questions were administered using Audio Computer-Assisted Self Interview (ACASI). Some questions are similar to, but slightly different from, questions from prior years.
