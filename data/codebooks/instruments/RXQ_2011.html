LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Prescription Medication and Select OTC Drugs (RXQ)

Years of Content: 2011

First Published: February 2012

Last Revised: May 2013

Subject: Prescription medications with or without bottles and Non-prescription OTC drugs

Record Source(s): Modified questions from dietary supplementation and prescription medication section in NHANES

Data File: rxq_2011.sas7bdat

Component Description

Prescription medications and select OTC drugs (RXQ) provides specific information relating to any medications which respondents might be taking.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The RXQ section was completed during the first interview of a subject at the subject’s home by a trained interviewer using CAPI.

Data Processing

The RXQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home personal interview portion of the SHOW study (Time 1), and T1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. Further data cleaning with regard to the name of the medications is needed in order to use this dataset.
