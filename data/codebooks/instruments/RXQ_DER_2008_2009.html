Title: Derived Variables - Prescription Medication and Select OTC Drugs (RXQ_DER)

Years of Content: 2008-2009

First Published: April 2013

Last Revised: May 2013

Subject: Prescription medication and OTC drugs history

Raw Data Source(s): RXQ, HHQ1, KEY

Data File: rxq_der_2008_2009.sas7bdat

Component Description

These are the derived variables for the Prescription Medication and Select OTC Drugs which is a part of the in-home personal interview portion of the SHOW study (Time 1).

Analytic Notes

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 1 portion of the SHOW study and T1 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data. All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calculations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data. Additional details are available upon request.
