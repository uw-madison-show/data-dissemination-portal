Component Description

The Sensory and Dental (SDQ) provides specific information relating to vision, hearing, noise exposure, dental status.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SDQ section was completed during the first interview of a subject at the subject’s home by a trained interviewer using PAPI from June 2008-November 2008 and using CAPI starting in December 2008.

Data Processing and Editing

SDQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Starting in December 2008 these logical tests were incorporated into the CAPI programming.   

known Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in home personal interview portion of the SHOW study (Time 1), and T1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.

 

Codes for ansewer option in SDQ062 (SDQ062_A to SDQ062_I) and SDQ063 (SDQ063_2 to SDQ063_10) were changed at some point in 2008.  Therefore, furthere data cleaning work should be done to use SDQ062 and SDQ063 data.

 

This dataset does not have information on SDQ063_1 (age of onset of tubes in ears) because of initial data processing error.

 

Codes for answer option in SDQ342 in 2008-2009 were different from those in 2010 dataset although question itself is the same.  Therefore, answer code of SDQ342 in 2008 and 2009 was changed into the way answer was coded in 2010 data.   No further concern is needed for SDQ342.

 

Answer option and skip pattern in SDQ121 was changed in 2010.
