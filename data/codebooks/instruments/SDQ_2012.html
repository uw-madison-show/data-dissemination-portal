Data File: sdq_2012.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Sensory and Dental (SDQ) provides specific information relating to vision, hearing, noise exposure, dental status.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SDQ section was completed during the first interview of a subject at the subject’s home by a trained interviewer.

Data Processing

SDQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. Starting in December 2008 these logical tests were incorporated into the CAPI programming.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in home personal interview portion of the SHOW study (Time 1), and T1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. SDQ140 (“Have you ever fired a gun?”) is no longer being collected.
