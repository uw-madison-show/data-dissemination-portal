Subject: Sensory and Dental History

Record Source(s): SHOW original questions and some modified from NHANES, NEI, BFRSS, NHIS, Hispanic Community Health Study/Study of Latinos Hearing Exam, ARIC, and Beaver Dam Eye Study

Data File: sdq_2013.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Sensory and Dental (SDQ) provides specific information relating to vision, hearing, noise exposure, dental status.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SDQ section was completed during the first interview of a subject at the subject’s home by a trained interviewer.

Data Processing

SDQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. Starting in December 2008 these logical tests were incorporated into the CAPI programming.

Analytic Notes

Significant revisions were made to simplify the vision and hearing sections for 2014. For the vision section, questions pertaining to difficulty driving were added (SDQ031-SDQ033) while history of eye problems were removed. In the hearing section, history of hearing and balance problems were removed; noise exposure is no longer assessed. Slight wording changes were also made to several of the questions--specifically SDQ320, SDQ321, and SDQ340--when compared to those used in previous cohorts (2011 and earlier), but are not anticipated to affect responses. Changes listed above are not exhaustive. Please see annotated questionnaire for additional details.

These data were collected as part of the in home personal interview portion of the SHOW study (Time 1), and T1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
