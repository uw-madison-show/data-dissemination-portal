Years of Content: 2013

Subject: Sensory and Dental History

Raw Data Source(s): SDQ

Data File: sdq_der_2013.sas7bdat

Component Description

These are the derived variable(s) for the Sensory and Dental (SDQ) module administered during Time 1 (home visit) portion of the SHOW survey.

Analytic Notes

Conversion of questions with "Enter all the apply" responses to dichotomous variables was handled differently than in previous cohorts. When these questions were skipped due to a lead-in question, corresponding dichotomous variables are set to missing instead of "[2] No". Use caution on combining with data from previous cohorts.

These data were collected as part of the in home personal interview portion of the SHOW study (Time 1), and T1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
