Subject: General Health Status, including Physical and Mental Component

Record Source(s): SF-12v2™ Health Survey Standard Version  from Quality Metric Health Outcomes™

Data File: sf12_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The QA section is SF-12v2 standard health survey and provides information relating to individual’s perceived health status, including the physical component summary (PCS) and mental component summary (MCS) scores.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants 18 years and older are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The QA section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The QA section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). In previous years the SF12 questionnaire was called QA.
