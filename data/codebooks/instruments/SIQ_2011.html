Data File: siq_2011.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Screening and Immunization section (SIQ) provides specific information relating to common screening tests and vaccinations that have been recommended for different age groups by different health organizations.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SIQ section was completed during the first interview of a subject at the subject’s home by a trained interviewer using CAPI.

Data Processing

SIQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center. 

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home personal interview portion of the SHOW study (Time 1), and T1 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
