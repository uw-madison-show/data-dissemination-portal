LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Sleep Habits and Problems for Minors (SLM)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: May 2015

Last Revised: August 2015

Subject: Sleep habits, Sleep disorders and Excessive daytime sleepiness in minors

Record Source(s): Modified from NHANES Sleep questionnaire, Epworth Sleepiness questionnaire and other sources

Data File: slm_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The SLM section provides specific information relating to minors' sleep patterns and disorders related to sleep.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 0 to 17 were eligible for this section. For minors ages 0 to 12, this section is completed by an adult proxy.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SLM section was administered during the in-home portion of the survey by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program. Data collection occurred from July 2014 to January 2015.

Data Processing

The SLM section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the in-home intervirew portion of the SHOW study and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). This questionnaire is new in 2014.The Epworth Sleepiness Scale was modified slightly for use with minors by changing the wording of one question that referenced alcohol consumption.

References

Johns MW. A New Method for Measuring Daytime Sleepiness: The Epworth Sleepiness Scale. Sleep. 1991;14(6):540–545.
