LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Sleep Habits and Problems (SLP)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: July 2015

Last Revised: August 2015

Subject: Sleep habits, sleep disorders and excessive daytime sleepiness

Record Source(s): Modified from NHANES Sleep questionnaire, Epworth Sleepiness questionnaire and other sources

Data File: slp_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The SLP section provides specific information relating to individuals’ sleep patterns and disorders related to sleep.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18+ were eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SLP section was included in a booklet of self-administered questionnaires provided to study participants at the end of the first in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The SLP section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the self-administered portion of the SHOW study (SAQ) and sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use).

References

Johns MW. A New Method for Measuring Daytime Sleepiness?: The Epworth Sleepiness Scale. Sleep. 1991;14(6):540–545.
