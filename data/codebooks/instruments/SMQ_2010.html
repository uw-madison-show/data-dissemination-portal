LOCATOR RECORD:

 

Title: Smoking history and exposure to smoke (SMQ)

Years of Content: 2010

First Published: February 2011

Last Revised: N/A

Access and Use Constraints: None

Subject: History of smoking

Data File: SMQ_2010.sas7bdat

 

Component Description

The SMQ focuses on respondents’ smoking histroy.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SMQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the exam center portion (Time 3) of the SHOW study.

Data Processing and Editing

The SMQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Known Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the exam center portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
