LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Smoking history and exposure to smoke (SMQ)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: April 2015

Last Revised: August 2015

Subject: History of smoking

Record Source(s): SMQ

Data File: smq_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The SMQ focuses on respondents' smoking history

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18+ are eligible for this section

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SMQ section was included in a a booklet of self-administered questionniares provided to study participants at the end of the first in home interview to complete and return to SHOW.

Data Processing

The SMQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data inpt errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center

Analytic Notes

This is the first year that SMQ was a self-administered paper questionnaire. It changed modes this year and was previously administered by an interviewer using an audio computer-assisted self interview (A-CASI) program during the exam center portion of the SHOW study in years past. Also, this year we dropped majority of the questions about quitting intentions and methods used for quitting.

References
