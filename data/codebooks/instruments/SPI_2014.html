Component Description

The Spirometry (SPI) section provides information on exclusion criteria and factors that may affect the results, as well as the values of spirometry testing.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 18 years old and older are eligible for this section. Participants are ineligible for spirometry testing if they respond "YES" to any of the following questions: SPI002, SPI006, SPI008, SPI012, SPI014, SPI016 or SPI018.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SPI section was completed during the in-home interview and was administered by a trained interviewer using computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) between June 2014 and February 2015. In previous years, the SPI section was administered during the exam center visit that it is no longer a part of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

The SPI section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

Analyses interpreted with respect to the target population should incorporate the sampling weights and the complex survey design (see the Analytic Guidelines for more information).
