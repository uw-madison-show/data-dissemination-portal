Component Description

These are the derived variables for the spirometry module administered during the in-home portion of the SHOW survey.

Analytic Notes

Normal population (SPI_NORMAL3) is determined based on self-report of the following conditions: cigarette smoking ever (SMQ020), ever diagnosis of asthma (HHQ210), ever diagnosis of emphysema or chronic bronchitis (HHQ230_R2). In previous years, normal population (SPI_NORMAL) was also determined based on persistent cough or wheezing but this year questions about these conditions were not included in the survey.

Observations are considered valid if 2 readings are within 10% of the maximum reading.

Population-predicted values for respondents who reported Hispanic ethnicity were derived from estimates from the Mexican-American population; no population-predicted values were calculated for respondents who indicated a race of "Other".

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the in-home visit and appropriate weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use).

All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Shorthand SAS calculations have been included in this codebook for some variables.
