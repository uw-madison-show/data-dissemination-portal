Years of Content: 2014

First Published: July 2015

Last Revised: August 2015

Subject: Screen time in minors

Record Source(s):

Data File: stm_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The STM section provides specific information relating to minors' sedentary behavior and screen time usage.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 3 to 17 were eligible for this section. For minors ages 3 to 11, this section is completed by an adult proxy.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The STM section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program in the in-home portion of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

The STM section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.  Further data processing and cleaning were done by staff of the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

This questionnaire was added in 2014. Sampling weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the 2014 analytic guidelines for information on what weights to use).
