Component Description

These are the derived variables for the Short Test of Functional Health Literacy in adults administered during the Time 3 (survey center) portion of the SHOW survey. The STOFHLA Large Print (14 point) English Version was self-administered by respondents using photocpied masters and scored as directed using the STOFHLA Directions for Administration, Scoring & Technical Data published by Peppercorn Books & Press Inc.

Analytic Notes

All derived variables in this dataset used variables collected during the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study and T3 survey weights should be used when analyzing these data. All processing was completed using SAS version 9.3. Short hand SAS calculations have been included to help researchers recreate these variables from the raw data. Additional details are available upon request. Notes from the administration of the STOFHLA examination (STOFHLA_NOTES) were used to help determine if the test should be scored. In a few instances a STOFHLA exam was not scored due to inconsistnacies with administration. Please review the STOFHLA_NOTES from the raw STOFHLA dataset for more information. If, after seven minutes of examination the exam was left blank the respondent received a score of 0 and were placed in the "Inadequate Functional Health Literacy" group.

References

Baker DW, Parker RM, Williams MV, Gazmarian JA, Nurss JR (1999). Development of a brief test to measure functional health literacy. Patient Education and Counseling, 38, 22-42.
