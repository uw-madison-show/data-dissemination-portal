Title: Derived Variables - Stress

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: March 2015

Last Revised: August 2015

Subject: Stress, Anxiety, and Depression

Raw Data Source(s): Stress

Data File: stress_der_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

These are the derived variables for the STRESS module included in the self-administered questionnaire portion of the SHOW survey.

Analytic Notes

Scores and related statistics were calculated for all respondents who answered all 7 questions for each of the stress, anxiety, and depression constructs. Interpretation of values obtained from incomplete surveys should be taken in the context.

References

Lovibond, S.H. & Lovibond, P.F. (1995). Manual for the Depression Anxiety Stress Scales. (2nd. Ed.) Sydney: Psychology Foundation.
