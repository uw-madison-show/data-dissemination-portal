Component Description

The Sexual Behavior (SXQ) module provides information on sexual history and behaviors.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SXQ module was administed on paper or through ACASI.

Data Processing and Editing

The SXQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Known Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the Exam Center portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
