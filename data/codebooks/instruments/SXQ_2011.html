Component Description

The Sexual Behavior (SXQ) module provides information on sexual history and behaviors.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SXQ module was administered through ACASI.

Data Processing

The SXQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

Those who reported that they had not had sex before were not asked about condom usage. These data were collected as part of the Exam Center portion of the SHOW study (Time 3) and T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data. Females were only asked SXQ020 after February 20, 2011.
