Component Description

The Sexual Identity (SXQ) module provides information on participant's sexual identity.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants 18 years and older are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The SXQ module was administed through audio computer assisted self-interview (ACASI) in the in-home interview portion of the SHOW survey between July 2014 and January 2015.

Data Processing

The SXQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of data collection and entry by the University of Wisconsin Survey Center.

Analytic Notes

The SXQ questionnaire completely changed between the years 2013 and 2014. The only question that is the same between the years is SXQ010. Questions: SXQ011, SXQ012, SXQ013, SXQ014 were added. Sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use).
