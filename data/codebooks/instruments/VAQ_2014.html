LOCATOR RECORD

Title: Verification of Age (VAQ)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: August 2015

Last Revised: August 2015

Subject: Verification of subject's age.

Record Source(s):

Data File: vaq_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The VAQ section asks the subject his/her age to verify or correct the age information collected during the screening process. If the subject refuses to provide his/her age, then the interview is terminated.

Eligible Sample

All subjects.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The VAQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program during the in-home portion of the SHOW study.

Data Processing

The VAQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.
