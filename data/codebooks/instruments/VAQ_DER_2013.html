Title: Derived Variables - Verification of Age (VAQ_DER)

Years of Content: 2013

First Published: March 2014

Last Revised: April 2014

Subject: Date of Birth

Raw Data Source(s): Verification of Age (VAQ) and Household Screener (SCQ)

Data File: vaq_der_2013.sas7bdat

Component Description

This instrument contains the most accurate birthdate information that we collected for each subject.

Analytic Notes

All 447 subjects in 2013 provided a complete date of birth in the VAQ. If birthdate was collected in both the VAQ and SCQ for a subject, then the birthdate from VAQ was used, with two exceptions. In 2013 there were two subjects with discrepant birthdate information from the VAQ and SCQ, and we used the SCQ data because it was more consistent with external data sources and interviewer notes.
