Component Description

The Weight History (WHQ) section provides specific information relating to the actual and perceived weight of the respondent over his/her lifetime.Â  Methods used to lose or maintain weight are also ascertained.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants ages 21-74 are eligible for this section.

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The WHQ section is completed during the Time 3 (survey center) visit. It is completed via CASI directly on a laptop by the respondent.

Data Processing and Editing

The WHQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of cleaning by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Known Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the Time 3 portion of the SHOW study T3 sample weights should be used when analyzing these data.
