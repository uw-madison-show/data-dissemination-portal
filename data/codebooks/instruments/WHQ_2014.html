Title: Weight History (WHQ)

Years of Content: 2014

First Published: March 2015

Last Revised: July 2015

Subject: Weight History

Record Source(s): Modified from NHANES

Data File: whq_2014.sas7bdat

Component Description

The Weight History (WHQ) section provides specific information relating to the actual and perceived weight of the respondent over his/her lifetime. Methods used to lose or maintain weight are also ascertained.

Eligible Sample

SHOW participants age 18+

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The WHQ section was administered by an interviewer using a computer-assisted personal interview (CAPI) program.

Data Processing

The WHQ section data were systematically reviewed for logical, processing, and data input errors during the process of cleaning by the Survey of the Health of Wisconsin.

Analytic Notes

These data were collected as part of the CAPI portion of the SHOW study and appropriate sample weights should be used when analyzing these data (see the 2014 Analytic Guidelines for information on what weights to use). Some questions are similar to, but slightly different from, prior years WHQ questions.
